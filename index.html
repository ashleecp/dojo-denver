About the Dojo

Led by volunteering mentors, young people between 7 and 17 learn how to code, develop websites, apps, programs, games and much more. In addition to learning to code, members meet like minded people, show off what they've been working on and so on. CoderDojo makes development and learning to code a fun, sociable and awesome experience.

CoderDojo also puts a strong emphasis on open source and free software, and has a strong network of members and volunteers globally.

CoderDojo has just one rule: "Above All: Be Cool", bullying, lying, wasting people's time and so on is uncool.

We are seeking facillitating and mentoring volunteers! Our size is limitted by the number of contributing volunteers. Please help us grow and foster a safe community where kids can have fun and learn.

Events

All events are free. Tickets are limited by the number of volunteering mentors who assist in facillitating the Dojo. Tickets can be reserved through EventBrite, embeded on the right side of this page. If you are interested in mentoring and growing the Dojo, please talk to the organizers.

If attending please bring a laptop & a parent (ages 12 & under).
